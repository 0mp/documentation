\subsection*{getminor: Get the minor device number from a device structure}

\subsubsection*{Calling convention}

\begin{description}
\item[\registerop{rd}] Minor device number
\end{description}

\subsubsection*{Description}

The \subroutine{getminor} subroutine returns the minor number from a
device structure.

\subsubsection*{Constraints}

The \subroutine{getminor} subroutine is only available on Illumos and
systems derivice from OpenSolaris.

\subsubsection*{Failure modes}

This subroutine has no run-time failure modes beyond its constraints.
